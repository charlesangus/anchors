## Decisions Made
- QShortcut Ctrl+Z scoped to dialog for Reset undo rather than QUndoStack — simpler, only one undo level needed
- `_publish_path` read once at `__init__` time from `os.environ.get("ANCHORS_SITE_CONFIG", "")` — no need for dynamic re-check
- `_on_publish_naming` flushes live field values to prefs_module before calling publish() — ensures published file reflects what user sees
- `_FRAME_TOKEN_PATTERN` compiled locally in `_update_naming_validity_indicator` to avoid circular-import risk from anchor.py (documented in Phase 15 summary)
