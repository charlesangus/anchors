<decisions_made>

- `with nuke.lastHitGroup():` context managers are the correct fix — Qt signals fire
  in Root context, so `nuke.thisGroup()` has already reset by the time `invoke()` runs.
  `lastHitGroup()` persists correctly across the Qt boundary.
- `nuke.zoom()` replaces `nuke.zoomToFitSelected()` — the latter always targets the
  root DAG; the former targets the focused panel (confirmed by live test).
- Group View zoom limitation accepted — no public Nuke API to pan a specific floating
  panel. Alt+A from Group View surfaces correct anchors but doesn't pan the panel.

</decisions_made>

get_items()/invoke() methods. Both approaches work together now.
